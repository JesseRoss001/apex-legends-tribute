Lore : A brief background

Gibraltar , the jolly behemoth with a penchant for wild escapades, emerges as the love child of adventure-seeking SARAS (Search and Rescue Association of Solace) volunteers. From his early days, he exhibited an uncanny knack for extricating folks from perilous predicaments that were all too commonplace in the Outlands. But it wasn't until his adolescence that the true meaning of safeguarding others truly hit home. Picture this: one fine day, he and his beau swiped his old man's motorcycle, went on an audacious joyride, only to find themselves ensnared in the clutches of a treacherous mudslide. It took the heroic efforts of his parents to rescue them, with dear old dad bidding farewell to an arm in the process. Gibraltar, never one to forget a debt, made it his life's mission to lend a hand to those in dire straits.


The Apex Games, however, altered the game plan. Over the years, many of Gibraltar's chums and comrades plunged headlong into the Games, lured by the allure of moolah, fame, and glory, some never to return. Gibraltar, spurred by a desire to keep his pals out of harm's way, decided to throw his hat in the ring. For the first time ever, his rescue skills and rebellious spirit found common ground. Now, he's the veritable rockstar of the Apex Games, willingly stepping into the line of fire to shield his squad and send opponents scampering for cover.


Oh, the sights he's seen, both within and outside the Games, enough to give his optimistic outlook a run for its money. From taking the fall for a blunder, only to reunite with the love of his life and try to make amends, to witnessing pals thrust themselves into one dicey situation after another, and witnessing ancient rivalries unravel in uproarious fashion. Some audacious competitors even tried to rattle his composure just for the kicks. But Gibraltar, he's as unshakeable as a mountain; they can try to budge him, but all they'll get is an infectious grin and a hearty laugh.


Jesse Ross
